Together with STERNA | PAU I developed a web-based audiowalk with AR elements.

Around the topic of time, the audience is invited to wander around Dortmund's Ostpark.

It's live here https://vonanderenzeiten.sternapau.de/ but you need to be inside the park to access the content ;)

You can find the code here https://github.com/lislis/ostpark-walk

More at https://www.sternapau.de/projekte/die-zeit-ist-rund-at/
