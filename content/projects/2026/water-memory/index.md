This is a collabortation between [Madhumita Nandi](https://www.madhumitanandi.com/) and myself.

Starting from Madhu's experiences as migrant, we explore water as our shared holder of memory. The connection of drained swamp lands in Kolkata and Berlin leads us to power structures in and around water, and how we relate to it.

This is a work in progress, where we collect memories aroudn water and build an interactive water pump piece to explore the watery archive playfully.
