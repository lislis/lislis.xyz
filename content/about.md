Lis is a creative technologist, activist and artist based in Berlin. Coming from software development, lis has a strong focus on tech infrastructure and open source software but also explores playful interactions, interfaces and video games.

They gives workshops and trainings around all things programming, digital self-defense and online privacy. In their artistic work and collaborations you can see complex (cyber-) feminist themes merge with simple mechanics in installative or digital forms.

Lis is an active member of the [Heart of Code](https://heartofcode.org/en/), a feminist hackspace for women, inter, non-binary, trans and agender people in Berlin.
